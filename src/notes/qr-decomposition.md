The QR Decomposition factors a matrix A into an orthonormal matrix Q and an upper triangular matrix R.

```math
A = QR
```

The QR Decomposition is often used to solve the linear least squares problem.

```math
Ax = b \\\\
QRx = b \\\\ 
x = R^{-1}Q^Tb \\\\ 
```
